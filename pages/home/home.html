Welcome to the Katipunan Bank!

The Katipunan Bank is here to help its fellow kapwa with the newly developed credit card balance transfer calculator application.
Created by the proponents of the bank, the calculator in the next tab only needs the bank tranfer amount as well as the payment
terms in order to calculate three different things: total interest payable, monthly payment, and total amount. With this, knowing
your credit card balance has never been easier! Goodluck fellow kapwa, and mabuhay to all Filipinos!
